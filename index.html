MEMORIAL DAY 2016

We recently had our first exciting APV Memorial Day family pool party and barbecue event at our new community activities campus which played host to over 700 happy residents! We held pool floaty races and hugely popular boat build regatta, while hundreds of residents were thrilled sliding down the two giant water slides that were set up within the field just outside our pool deck area. For a solid 3 hours during the event, hungry APV families consumed hundreds of plates filled with delicious BBQ hamburgers, grilled hot dogs and sides. In fact, 13 large tubs of ice cream seemingly vanished into thin air as we offered free all-you-can-eat ice cream to cool down our residents.
